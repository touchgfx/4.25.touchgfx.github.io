Choose the right framebuffer strategy

In this video, we explore the concept of framebuffers in TouchGFX. Choosing the right framebuffer strategy is crucial to optimize memory layout and maximize performance on your hardware.

We will cover three different strategies:

  • Double framebuffer
  • Single framebuffer
  • Emulated framebuffer

Please note that the behavior of these strategies varies depending on whether the display has Graphic RAM (GRAM) or not.
